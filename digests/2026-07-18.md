## 3. ALICE: Learning a General-Purpose Pathology Foundation Model from Vision, Vision-Language, and Slide-Level Experts

- **事实**：ALICE is a unified foundation model for computational pathology, trained through multi-stage agglomerative distillation from vision, vision-language, and slide-level experts. The paper is published on arXiv.
- **技术视角**：The multi-stage agglomerative distillation approach may enable ALICE to integrate complementary expertise across different spatial scales and pretraining objectives, potentially outperforming single-backbone models.
- **行业影响**：This work addresses the fragmentation of foundation models in computational pathology by unifying multiple expert backbones, which could streamline deployment in clinical workflows.
- **值得关注**：Future work may benchmark ALICE against existing pathology foundation models on downstream tasks such as diagnosis and prognosis. Next signal: publication of benchmark results or open-source release.
- 证据：[ALICE: Learning a General-Purpose Pathology Foundation Model from Vision, Vision-Language, and Slide-Level Experts](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.09526v1)

## 5. Seeing is Free, Speaking is Not: Uncovering the True Energy Bottleneck in Edge VLM Inference

- **事实**：一篇 arXiv 论文（2607.09520v1）于 2026-07-10 发布，研究边缘设备上视觉语言模型（VLM）的能耗瓶颈，发现视觉处理并非主要能耗来源，而语言生成（解码）才是真正的能耗瓶颈。
- **技术视角**：VLM 在边缘设备上的能耗优化重点应从减少视觉 token 转向优化语言解码过程，例如通过更高效的解码架构或硬件加速。
- **行业影响**：这一发现可能影响边缘 AI 芯片设计，促使厂商在语言解码单元上投入更多资源，而非仅关注视觉处理。
- **值得关注**：未来可能出现针对边缘 VLM 语言解码的专用硬件或算法优化，如稀疏解码或量化技术。
- 证据：[Seeing is Free, Speaking is Not: Uncovering the True Energy Bottleneck in Edge VLM Inference](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.09520v1)
